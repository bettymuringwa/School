Documents Required

  1. IGCSE/ BGCSE Transcripts.
  2. Previous school teacher recommendation letter.
  3. ID / Passport certified copy for the student and parent/guardian.
  4. Certified copy of student study permit.
  5. Birth certificate for the student.
  6. Two passport size photos.

Admission Process

  1. The Admissions office will notify parents/guardians on the admission status.

Curriculum — AS and A2 Level

At A-Level students select minimum four subjects to pursue for their A-Level exams.

Electives (choose minimum four): Maths, English, Further Maths, Accounting, Economics, Business Studies, Enterprise, Biology, Physics, Chemistry, English Literature, French, Setswana, Art, Agriculture, Extended Maths, Geography, History
