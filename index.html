Who We Are


At Livingstone Kolobeng College, our mission is to nurture successful leaders with competence and excellence. We deliver quality education through knowledgeable, experienced teachers in a safe, supportive, and stimulating environment where students grow with confidence.
We proudly offer IGCSE (Form 1–5) and A-Level (AS & A2) programmes, with a proven track record of producing some of the best results in Botswana and even Top in the World achievers. Every learner is guided to reach their full potential, and we take pride in their outstanding accomplishments. We look forward to welcoming you into the LKC community.
