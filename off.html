Steps To Follow


Admissions

Livingstone Kolobeng College welcomes students who are eager to learn and grow. Our enrollment process is simple, transparent, and designed to help families make the best choice for their child’s education.

Academics

Our academic programs include IGCSE (Form 1–5) and A-Level (AS & A2), designed to provide students with the knowledge and skills to excel locally and internationally. We take pride in producing top achievers every year.

Sports

Beyond the classroom, students enjoy a variety of sporting activities such as soccer, netball, athletics, and more. Our sports program encourages teamwork, discipline, and healthy living while nurturing talent and school spirit.
