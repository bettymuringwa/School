Who We Are


At Livingstone Kolobeng College, our mission is to nurture successful leaders with competency and excellence. The school delivers quality education through knowledgeable, competent, and experienced teachers. By providing a safe, supportive, and stimulating environment, students are encouraged to learn and grow with confidence. Every learner is seen as having the potential to succeed, and our role is to guide them toward achieving their goals. We take great pride in the achievements of our students and look forward to welcoming you into the LKC community.

Your Path to World-Class Education

Established in 2010, Livingstone Kolobeng College is committed to shaping bright futures through quality education and a supportive learning environment. Our academic programs include IGCSE (Form 1 to Form 5) and A-Level studies (AS & A2), designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge they need to excel. Over the years, LKC has proudly produced outstanding results, with students ranking Top in the World and Top in Botswana for both IGCSE and A-Level examinations.

Academics

At Livingstone Kolobeng College, we offer a well-rounded curriculum with a strong focus on STEM, ICT, and innovation, while also empowering students in languages, business, and enterprise education. Our balanced approach ensures learners are fully prepared to excel academically and beyond.

To complement classroom learning, our students actively participate in competitions such as Mathematics Olympiads and the Botswana Stock Exchange Challenge. These opportunities provide valuable exposure and practical experience, building the skills and confidence needed to become future leaders and entrepreneurs.

Boarding Life at Its Best

Livingstone Kolobeng College offers boarding facilities that are more than just a place to stay—they are a nurturing home away from home. Designed for boys and girls from Form 1 to Form 6, our modern and safe residences provide an environment where students can grow academically, socially, and personally.

With attentive staff, cozy accommodations, and vibrant communal spaces, every student enjoys a supportive atmosphere. Here, friendships flourish, learning is encouraged, and memorable experiences are created every day. Our boarding facilities combine care, comfort, and community to ensure every student feels at home.


Sports & Activities

Sports are a cornerstone of life at Livingstone Kolobeng College. Through Physical Education (PE) classes, students gain hands-on training and develop skills that help them excel in athletics, rugby, soccer, basketball, and tennis. Many go on to represent the school in competitions, earning titles and awards, including as the celebrated Rugby Jaguars, our senior school rugby champions, and top performers in ISSA events.

Participating in sports cultivates teamwork, discipline, resilience, and leadership, while promoting physical fitness, preparing students for success both on and off the field.
