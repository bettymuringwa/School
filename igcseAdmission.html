Curriculum — Form 1 & Form 2 (Junior School)

Junior school subjects are compulsory and help students determine the academic path for their IGCSE exams. In Form 1 and Form 2 we prepare students for the Cambridge Checkpoint exams which guide IGCSE subject selection.

Subjects: Mathematics, English, Combined Sciences, ICT, Elective Practical (Art / Agriculture), Physical Education, Accounts, Economics, Business Studies

Curriculum — Form 3 to Form 5 (Senior School)

At Form 3 students select eight subjects to pursue for their IGCSE exams.

Compulsory Subjects: Mathematics (Extended or Core), English (1st Language or 2nd Language)

Electives (choose six): Accounting, Economics, Business Studies, Enterprise, Biology, Physics, Chemistry, English Literature, French, Setswana, Art, Agriculture, Extended Maths, Geography, History
