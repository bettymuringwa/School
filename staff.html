Our Staff



Dedicated Teaching Staff

At Livingstone Kolobeng College, we offer a well-rounded curriculum with a strong focus on STEM, ICT, and innovation, while also empowering students in languages, business, and enterprise education. Our balanced approach ensures learners are fully prepared to excel academically and beyond.
To complement classroom learning, our students actively participate in competitions such as Mathematics Olympiads and the Botswana Stock Exchange Challenge. These opportunities provide valuable exposure and practical experience, building the skills and confidence needed to become future leaders and entrepreneurs.

At Livingstone Kolobeng College, we are proud of our team of 40 qualified and experienced teachers who are passionate about guiding students toward excellence. Each educator is well-trained, certified, and committed to producing the best results while inspiring learners to aim high and achieve their goals.
